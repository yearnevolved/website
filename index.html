What is Yearn Evolved?

It is a 2-token model that aims to achieve what DeFi was supposed to be.
YEVO will be the governance token and 2nd token will be the fuel token for the farming pools.

Providing utility to the farmed token is our main focus. We do not want the community to farm, dump on the market and move on to next. This will be achieved when 2nd token is in circulation, gaining utility in a working product. Building an AMM and using the token as the LP fees.

  • An AMM that focus on interoperability and scalability
  • Users can swap different chain tokens and not only ERC-20
  • A community platform for contribution.
